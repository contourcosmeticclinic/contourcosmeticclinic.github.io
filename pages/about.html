Hair TREATMENT

At Contour Cosmetic Clinic, we offer advanced hair restoration solutions designed to combat hair thinning, hair loss, and scalp concerns with proven, medically backed treatments. Our team of skilled trichologists and surgeons personalize each treatment plan to suit your unique hair goals. Whether you're considering PRP therapy, laser scalp treatments, or a permanent solution like Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE) hair transplant, we ensure results that look natural and last.
