Why Hair Loss Happens

95% of men and 40% of women experience pattern baldness
  • DHT hormone causes follicles to shrink and stop producing hair
  • Genetics, stress, medical conditions, and hormones contribute to hair loss
  • Early intervention leads to better treatment outcomes

Hair Transplant: The Permanent Solution

Hair transplantation moves healthy follicles from dense areas to thinning spots

These transplanted follicles are genetically resistant to DHT, ensuring permanent, natural-looking results.

Treatment Options

FUE (Follicular Unit Extraction) - Most Popular

FUE Benefits
  • Virtually invisible tiny scars
  • Faster recovery (3-5 days)
  • Wear any hairstyle, including short cuts
  • Minimal discomfort
  • Higher success rates

FUT (Strip Method) - For Large Sessions

FUT Details
  • Removes strip of scalp, leaves linear scar
  • Longer recovery period
  • More grafts in single session
  • More affordable option
