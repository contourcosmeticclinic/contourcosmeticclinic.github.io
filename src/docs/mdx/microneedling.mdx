## What is Microneedling (Collagen Induction Therapy)?

Microneedling, also known as Collagen Induction Therapy (CIT), is a minimally invasive cosmetic procedure that harnesses your body's natural healing processes. It involves using a device equipped with tiny, sterile needles to create controlled micro-injuries or micro-channels on the skin's surface.

These microscopic punctures are invisible to the naked eye but are sufficient to trigger your skin's wound-healing response. This response initiates a cascade of benefits:

- **Collagen and Elastin Production:** The primary goal is to stimulate the production of new collagen and elastin – the essential proteins responsible for skin's firmness, elasticity, and youthful structure.
- **Enhanced Absorption:** The micro-channels created allow for significantly better absorption of topical serums, growth factors, and other active ingredients, maximizing their effectiveness.
- **Cell Turnover:** Promotes the shedding of old, damaged skin cells and the generation of new, healthier cells.

4. **Post-Treatment Care:** You will experience some redness and mild swelling immediately after the treatment, similar to a sunburn. This typically subsides within 24-48 hours. Our team will provide comprehensive aftercare instructions, emphasizing gentle cleansing, moisturizing, and strict sun protection (SPF 30+ is crucial).
5. **Healing and Results:** Over the next few days, your skin may feel slightly rough or dry as it begins to heal. Within weeks, you will start to notice improvements in skin texture and tone. The full benefits of collagen remodeling will become more apparent over several months as new collagen develops.
6. **Recommended Sessions:** For optimal and lasting results, a series of 3-6 sessions, typically spaced 4-6 weeks apart, is usually recommended. Maintenance sessions may be advised annually.
